About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Michigan State University, advised by Dr. Josh Vermaas. My research focuses on leveraging **Molecular Simulations**, **Computational Chemistry/Biology**, and **Machine Learning** techniques to address challenges in **Drug Discovery**, **Drug Delivery**, and **Enzyme Engineering**.

Previously, I completed my PhD in Chemical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar, where my dissertation explored the role of charged residues in protein structural dynamics and adsorption under the guidance of Dr. Mithun Radhakrishna. I am passionate about applying computational methods to understand complex biological systems and contribute to scientific advancements.
