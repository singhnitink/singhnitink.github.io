HydroDisPred

Intrinsically Disordered Region Predictor

Citation: Singh, N. K., Bhardwaj, P., & Radhakrishna, M. (2023). Hydrophobicity—A Single Parameter for the Accurate Prediction of Disordered Regions in Proteins. Journal of Chemical Information and Modeling, 63(17), 5375-5383.

About HydroDisPred (HDP)

  • Algorithm: Uses hydrophobicity fraction (λ) to predict disordered regions
  • Critical Value: λC = 0.25 (regions with λ < 0.25 are disordered)
  • Segment Size: Δ = 10 residues
  • Accuracy: Comparable to machine learning approaches (PONDR, IUPred)
